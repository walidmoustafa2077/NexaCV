Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 24, 2024

At StructureFlow, we value the trust you place in our platform to handle your professional data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services. By using StructureFlow, you agree to the practices outlined in this document.

Data Collection

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including but not limited to:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and professional credentials.
  • User Content: Data entry for resumes, menus, or structured workflow documents created within the application.
  • Payment Information: Transactional details for premium subscriptions processed via our secure partners.

Usage

Your data is used to provide and enhance our high-integrity data entry experience. Specifically, we use your information to:

Service Provision

Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and security protocols to safeguard your information. All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is protected using Transport Layer Security (TLS).

Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel who require the information to perform their roles, and all such access is audited for integrity.

Cookies

StructureFlow uses essential cookies to manage your session and remember your preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising purposes without your explicit consent.

Cookie Management

You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, though some features may break.
